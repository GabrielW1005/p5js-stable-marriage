1) Choose any single man, have him propose to his top choice that has not yet rejected or dumped him.

2) If the woman he is proposing to is single, she accepts. If the woman is not single, she compares the man she is matched with to the man who is proposing and she chooses her preference.

3) Repeat steps 1-2 until there are no single men. This will always produce a stable matching.
